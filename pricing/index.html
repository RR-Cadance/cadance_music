Pricing questions

Do higher tiers unlock more features?

No — all tiers include the same Cadance features. Choose the tier that matches how much value you’re getting from Cadance in your teaching and business.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Cadance offers a 1‑month free trial. Just cancel 24 hours before the end of the trial to avoid being charged. No matter how many classes you teach each week, this should provide ample time to audition Cadance.

Will pricing be different in my country?

Often, yes. Apple sets local prices and currencies. The App Store always shows the final price before you start a trial or subscribe.

Can I switch or cancel later?

Yes — you can manage, switch, or cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple ID subscription settings.

Why a subscription instead of a one‑time purchase?

Subscriptions support continuous updates, new features, and long‑term maintenance so Cadance stays reliable for your classes year after year. If you want the longer explanation, read: Why Cadance is a subscription.

Is this tax deductible?

Many teachers treat subscriptions like Cadance as a business expense, but rules vary by country and by individual circumstances. This is not tax advice — please confirm with a tax professional.
