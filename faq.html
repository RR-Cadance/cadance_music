Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for teachers, musicians, and partners.

What is Cadance?

Cadance is a dance class music app built to keep classes moving with fast access, class‑friendly controls, and reliable playback. It’s the sequel to BarreNotes.

How can I try Cadance?

Download on the App Store: View Cadance on the App Store.

Why is there a new Cadance beta app?

To enable the integrated watchOS app and improve visibility in your app list, Cadance now uses a new app ID and TestFlight beta with a brighter icon. Because Apple treats this as a completely new app, it appears as a separate install.

You can safely delete the older, darker Cadance icon when you are ready. The only downside is that any music you imported into the previous beta will need to be imported again into the new one. This reset is a limitation of how app identifiers and TestFlight work, not something we can change.

Does Cadance support tempo change and repeats?

Yes. Tempo control and repeats are core to Cadance. Deeper guides are coming to How To.

Can I import my own music?

Yes, this is a big improvement over BarreNotes! We make it easy to push your music to Cadance via AirDrop or pull it from the cloud or a shared drive. See How To for Cadance features guide.

How does Cadance drive music sales?

Visit Musicians & Album Sellers to learn how our professional features transform owned music into essential teaching tools, creating meaningful demand for music ownership. See our featured partners and partnership program.

What professional features does Cadance offer?

Cadance provides tempo control (60-200%), smart start delays for positioning, session timers, seamless looping, and complete offline reliability. These professional capabilities require music ownership to unlock their full potential.

How can artists partner with Cadance?

Artists can join our partnership program to help their community discover music ownership benefits. We provide professional messaging templates, co-marketing support, and engagement strategies. See our current partners.

Do you track visitors or use analytics?

We use Microsoft Clarity for user behavior insights to improve the site experience. Read more in our Privacy Policy.

How do I contact support?

Email info.rondo@cadance.music and we’ll respond promptly.
