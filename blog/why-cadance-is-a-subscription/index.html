Why Cadance is a subscription

Dec 27, 2025

Cadance is built for professional teaching: tempo control that holds up, clean starts, reliable looping, and offline playback you can trust mid-class.

To keep that level of reliability year after year, we chose a subscription model.

BarreNotes was a long-running passion project

For over a decade, BarreNotes helped teachers run class smoothly. But keeping an app healthy over that long isn’t “one-and-done.” iOS changes, device changes, and App Store requirements never stop.

What subscriptions actually pay for

Our subscriptions aren’t about locking features behind tiers. They’re about making sure the app stays professional-grade and doesn’t become abandonware.

  • Ongoing iOS compatibility: updates for new OS versions, new devices, and Apple platform changes.
  • Reliability work: fixing edge cases that only show up in real studios, real Bluetooth speakers, real interruptions.
  • Feature improvements: polishing workflows so you spend less time tapping and more time teaching.
  • Long-term support: keeping the project sustainable so teachers can trust it as equipment.

We keep pricing teacher-friendly

Cadance plans start at $4.99/month (USD) with a 1-month free trial. You can see the full breakdown (monthly + yearly) on the pricing page.

See pricing & plans →

Try it the honest way

Try Cadance during the free trial in your real teaching week. If it earns a place in your workflow, keep it. If it doesn’t, cancel in Apple’s subscription settings.
