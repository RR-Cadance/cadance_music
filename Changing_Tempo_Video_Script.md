# Changing Tempo Video Script

## Voice Script (Narrator)

**[0-4 seconds]**
"Tap the 'slower' or 'faster' arrows to adjust tempo by 2% increments."

**[5-9 seconds]**
"For more precise control, tap and slide left or right across the tempo control."

**[10-14 seconds]**
"This gives you 1% precision across the full tempo range."

**[15-18 seconds]**
"To reset to original tempo, just tap the percentage value between the arrows."

---

## On-Screen Text Overlays

**[0-4 seconds]**
- **Arrow highlight**: Circle the slower/faster arrows
- **Text**: "Tap arrows = 2% steps"

**[5-9 seconds]**
- **Gesture highlight**: Show finger sliding motion
- **Text**: "Slide for full range control"

**[10-14 seconds]**
- **Precision indicator**: "1% precision"
- **Text**: "Full tempo range available"

**[15-18 seconds]**
- **Center value highlight**: Circle the percentage number
- **Text**: "Tap center to reset"

---

## Visual Demonstration Sequence

1. **[0-4s]** Show finger tapping slower arrow multiple times, tempo decreasing by 2% each tap (100% → 98% → 96% → 94%)
2. **[5-9s]** Show finger sliding left across control, tempo dropping smoothly to ~70%
3. **[10-11s]** Continue slide right, tempo increasing smoothly to ~150%
4. **[12-14s]** Show tempo range indicators (-50% and +100% endpoints)
5. **[15-18s]** Show finger tapping the center percentage value, tempo snapping back to 100%

---

## Technical Notes
- **Highlight UI elements** as they're mentioned (arrows, slider, percentage)
- **Show tempo changes in real-time** with music playing
- **Use visual indicators** (circles, arrows) to guide viewer attention
- **Keep demonstrations smooth** - no jarring cuts between actions
- **End with reset** to show complete control cycle
